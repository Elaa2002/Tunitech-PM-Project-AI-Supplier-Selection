Tunitech Project — Project Management Deliverables

This repository contains the project management deliverables developed for the academic group project:
“AI-Based Supplier & Distribution Selection Application” for Tunitech Manufacturing S.A.

The project applies formal PM concepts and PMBOK knowledge areas to the development of an AI-driven supplier evaluation and ranking system.

 Repository Contents
1. Project Charter

File: Project-Charter-TunitechSA.pdf
This document defines the project foundation, including:

Goal & background

Scope (in-scope & out-of-scope)

Stakeholders & roles

Project schedule & milestones

Budget & cost estimates

Risks & constraints

Team responsibilities

2. PMKAs Report (Project Management Knowledge Areas)

File: PMKAs-Report.pdf
A complete application of the 9 Project Management Knowledge Areas, covering:

Scope Management (WBS, requirements, acceptance criteria)

Schedule Management (Agile 4-cycle plan, Gantt chart, burndown, CFD)

Cost Management (cost baseline, HR & non-labor costing, reserves)

Quality Management (QA/QC, metrics, Ishikawa, PDCA, standards)

HR Management (RACI matrix, structure, training plan, motivation)

Communications Management (plan, reporting, channels, templates)

Risk Management (risk register, scoring, mitigation, matrices)

Procurement Management (make/buy analysis, supplier strategy)

Integration Management (project governance, baselines, change control)

This report demonstrates how all PMKAs work together to guide project execution and ensure delivery of the AI-based system.

 Project Context

Company: Tunitech Manufacturing S.A.
Project: Internal AI application for supplier/distributor scoring, ranking, and reliability prediction.
Objective: Improve procurement decision-making using data-driven and AI-powered insights.
Methodology: Agile (4 × 3-week sprints)
Deliverables: Clean dataset, prediction model, dashboard, training, documentation.
